About

“Lies, damned lies, and statistics”

Mark Twain, maybe

Statistics are beautiful and they can be used for truth or to mislead. The use of statistics can make any argument seem stronger, even if the statistics are misused. I frequently see statistics misused or used in less than ideal ways. This blog is a collection of things that bothered me enough to write a post about them.

There may even be statistics used wrong on this blog; we’re all human. If you see something say something.
